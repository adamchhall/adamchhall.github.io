CSCAR Consultant

As a CSCAR consultant, I helped graduate students and faculty resolve statistical questions related to their research. I also helped clients schedule appointments with more experienced consultants for clients with difficult problems requiring more specialized knowledge.

Common concerns that I helped with included:
